Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

MindPal is designed with your privacy at its core. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We store all your journal entries and mood data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

AI Features

Our AI-powered features (emotion analysis, reflection prompts, insights) run entirely on-device using Apple's CoreML framework. Your data never leaves your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is protected by Apple's privacy policy.

Face ID / Touch ID

Optional biometric lock uses Apple's Face ID/Touch ID APIs. We do not collect or store any biometric data.

Analytics

MindPal contains no analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Export

You can export all your data at any time via Settings. Your data belongs to you.

Contact
