DailyIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

DailyIQ is designed with your privacy as our priority. We collect minimal data necessary to provide you with intelligent scheduling services.

  • Task Data: Tasks, goals, and scheduling preferences you enter within the app
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage patterns to improve our AI scheduling algorithms
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for push notification delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing AI-powered daily scheduling and optimization
  • Sending timely reminders and notifications for scheduled tasks
  • Generating productivity insights and weekly reports
  • Improving app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

  • Local-first storage: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync
  • SQLite encryption for sensitive data
  • No third-party analytics or tracking without your consent

4. AI Processing

DailyIQ uses on-device machine learning for AI-powered scheduling. Your task data and goals are processed locally on your device to generate personalized daily schedules. We do not send your personal data to external AI servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics about app usage, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, DailyIQ may send push notifications to remind you of scheduled tasks and events. You can manage or disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

7. Widget Data

DailyIQ includes a widget extension. Widget data is shared through secure App Groups and remains on your device.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all your data at any time through the app's settings
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Disable any data collection features

9. Children's Privacy

DailyIQ is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
